Dota 2 news is an information website about the game Defense of the Ancients (Dota 2). Here you will understand how to start in the game. You also will know more about championships, international events, news, curiosities and more. Then, on this website you could get knowledge about all you need doing and knowing to be prepared to start you journey in Dota 2.

The word DOTA is an acronym of the game’s name. During the development of the second version of the game, it was decided by the gaming community that the game should be called Dota, as no one was using the game's full name. So, the number 2 was also added to refer to the new version. Thus, the name Dota 2 became official.

A curiosity of the game that it is free. You don’t need paying to play. All heroes are free. If you wish you can buy some skins and accessories for your hero, but it is just visual, it will not give you any advantage in the game. You can also buy the battle pass. With it you will unlock some features, exclusive skins and extra missions. In addition to increasing the XP and coins you earn on the game platform. This won't influence matches, but it can help you develop quickly as a player due to the new features that give you visibility about your performance.

Furthermore, Dota 2 is a desktop game. So, you will need have a good computer to play. You can find the minimum specs for your computer to run the game by clicking on the button download. When the page loads, scroll down until you find the system requirements.
